Our Team

We're a group of beer enthusiasts who, after one too many failed attempts at finding good breweries in new cities, decided enough was enough. We were tired of wandering aimlessly, searching for a cold beer oasis in a hot desert of mediocre bars. So we put our heads together, drank some more beer, and came up with the genius idea for BrewFinder. Now, we can help others avoid the same pitfalls we fell into and experience the joy of discovering great breweries wherever they go. Plus, we get to write off all our beer expenses as "research and development" now, so it's a win-win for everyone.
